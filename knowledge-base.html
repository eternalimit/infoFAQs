Knowledge Base: ClarityTM

What is ClarityTM?

ClarityTM is a custom-built conversational AI, based on GPT-4 Turbo technology, designed to help you write, edit, answer questions, and navigate complex topics. It combines a friendly, conversational approach with a powerful FAQ system for maximum clarity and confidence.

Key Features

  • AI-powered writing and editing
  • Dynamic FAQ and knowledge support
  • Security-focused guest mode
  • Watchdog protocol for safe and trusted conversations

How Does ClarityTM Work?

ClarityTM interacts through a simple, secure JSON-driven framework. It uses the latest GPT-4 Turbo conversational engine to provide responses and suggestions while respecting strict security and privacy rules.

You can access ClarityTM directly through the InfoFAQs platform or by visiting the external link below. It works seamlessly as both a writing assistant and a knowledge-based AI.
